Solar solutions

Off-grid and backup solar for unreliable grids

When power cuts are frequent or there is no grid at all, off‑grid solar with batteries keeps your essentials running on clean energy.

What is an off-grid solar system?

Panels charge a battery bank through a charge controller, and an inverter converts that stored energy to AC power for your home, independent of the utility grid.

  • • Perfect for farmhouses, remote sites or locations with no grid connection.
  • • Also used as a resilient backup for critical loads where outages are common.
  • • Requires careful sizing of panels and batteries to match your load and autonomy needs.

Is off-grid right for your site?

Articles like SolarSquare’s off‑grid guide explain that most city homes are better served by on‑grid systems, but off‑grid shines in specific scenarios.

No grid

Remote locations

Ideal when there is no distribution line nearby and diesel generators are expensive or noisy.

Poor reliability

Heavy power cuts

Use off‑grid or hybrid setups to keep key circuits – lights, router, fridge, pumps – running during long outages.

Higher cost
